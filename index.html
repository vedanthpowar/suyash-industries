Our World-Class Facilities

Advanced Machining Setup

  • VMC AMS 1060V - High precision vertical machining center.
  • CNC LMW LL20TL5 (2 Nos) - Advanced turning centers.
  • CNC ACE Micro J300 & Jobber XL - For critical component machining.
  • Conventional Setup - Universal Milling, 7-Feet Lathe, Radial Drilling.

Heavy-Duty Casting Capabilities

  • HPDC 900 TON (Yizumi) & HPDC up to 200 TON (Huda/Yizumi).
  • Hot Chamber Setup - 88 TON, 90 TON, 138 TON machines.
  • Gravity Die Casting - 4 GDC Machines with Melting holding furnaces.
  • Sand Casting - 1,280 Kg Highest Single Time Pouring Capacity.

Strict Quality & Inspection

  • NDT-DP Testing - Non-Destructive Dye Penetrant Testing setup.
  • Physical Testing - Tensile Test Machine & Scratch Hardness Machine.
  • Precision Gauges - Digital Height Gauges & customized Air Gauges.
  • Temperature Monitoring - Metal & Die Temperature measuring systems.
