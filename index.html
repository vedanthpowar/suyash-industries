Heavy-Duty Manufacturing Capabilities

🏗️

Pressure Die Casting (PDC)

Extensive setup including HPDC machines up to 900 TON (Yizumi/Huda), Hot Chamber machines up to 138 TON, and Cold Chamber setups.

🔥

GDC & Sand Casting

Unmatched capacity with a Highest Single Time Pouring of 1,280 Kg. Supported by 4 GDC machines and fully equipped Core Machine setups.

⚙️

Precision Machining

In-house VMC (AMS 1060V) and advanced CNC turning centers (LMW, ACE Micro) ensuring tight tolerances on complex geometries.

Quality & Inspection

Comprehensive NDT-DP testing, Tensile testing, Scratch hardness, Die/Metal temperature monitoring, and advanced Digital Height/Air Gauges.
